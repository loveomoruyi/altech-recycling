IT Asset Lifecycle Management

Our ITAD Management service provides end-to-end lifecycle management for your IT assets. From initial assessment and inventory tracking to secure data destruction and responsible disposition, we handle every step of the process.

We specialize in maximizing residual value through certified refurbishment and remarketing programs, ensuring your organization recovers the highest possible return on retired technology investments.

Data Center Decommissioning

Our team manages complete data center decommissioning projects, from servers and networking equipment to storage arrays and power infrastructure. Every asset is tracked with full chain-of-custody documentation.

Our ITAD Services Include

Asset Tracking

Complete inventory management with serial number tracking, condition assessment, and real-time reporting dashboards.

Data Destruction

NIST 800-88 compliant data sanitization with certificates of destruction for every device processed.

Remarketing

Professional refurbishment and resale through certified channels to maximize your return on retired assets.

Compliance Reporting

Detailed audit trails and compliance documentation for regulatory requirements including HIPAA, SOX, and PCI-DSS.
