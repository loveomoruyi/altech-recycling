Our Pickup & Logistics Service

Our logistics team coordinates the safe and efficient collection of your IT equipment. Whether you have a single pallet or an entire data center to decommission, we provide flexible pickup options tailored to your schedule and requirements.

All pickups include proper packaging materials, secure transportation, and full chain-of-custody documentation from your facility to ours.

Secure Transportation

GPS-tracked vehicles with locked compartments ensure your sensitive equipment is transported securely. Our drivers are background-checked and trained in handling IT equipment with care.
